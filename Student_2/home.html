UN Sustainable Development Goal: Life on Land

This website is dedicated to promoting and raising awareness about Goal 15: Life on Land, which aims to protect, restore, and promote the sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems, sustainably manage forests, combat desertification, and halt and reverse land degradation and halt biodiversity loss.

Key Focus Areas

  • Protecting animals: Learn about the importance of protecting various animal species to maintain biodiversity.
  • Preserving endangered species: Discover information about specific endangered species, their habitats, and ongoing conservation initiatives.
  • Conservation of forests: Understand the significance of forest conservation in maintaining ecological balance and supporting biodiversity.

What Visitors Can Explore

Visitors can explore interactive maps showcasing biodiversity hotspots, endangered species habitats, and conservation projects. Additionally, they can participate in ongoing campaigns aimed at supporting conservation efforts.

Campaigns

Explore ongoing campaigns related to biodiversity conservation, learn about their objectives, and find out how you can get involved.
